Privacy Policy for Google AdSense on the Dream Team Barber Shop page.

At Dream Team Barber Shop, we are committed to protecting the privacy and data of our visitors. Therefore, we have created this privacy policy to inform you about the collection and use of personal information on our website, specifically in relation to Google AdSense.

What information do we collect?

We may collect certain information from you when you visit our website. This information includes your IP address, browser settings, language, device type, and the pages you have visited. We collect this information to improve your browsing experience and provide personalized content.

How do we use Google AdSense?

At Dream Team Barber Shop, we use Google AdSense to display ads on our website. Google AdSense may collect information about your browsing activities, such as the ads you have viewed and clicked on.
Google AdSense uses cookies to deliver personalized ads based on your interests and previous browsing activities. These cookies may collect information such as your IP address, browser settings, language, device type, and the pages you have visited.
In addition, Google AdSense may track ad conversions, which means it can collect information about whether you have taken a desired action after clicking on an ad, such as making a purchase or completing a form. This helps us evaluate the effectiveness of our advertisements and optimize our marketing strategies.

How can you manage your privacy preferences?

You can manage your privacy preferences for Google AdSense and other providers through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to reject or block cookies, as well as delete existing cookies on your device.
Please note that by disabling or removing cookies, some features of our website may not work properly and the ads presented to you may be less relevant to your interests.
Additionally, you can use Google's ad preference tools to customize the ads shown to you based on your interests. Visit the "Personalized Ads" link on the Google Ads Settings page to learn more and make changes to your settings.
Your consent By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of information by Google AdSense as described in this privacy policy. If you do not agree to the collection and use of this information, we recommend that you do not use our website or adjust your browser settings to reject third-party cookies.
Privacy Policy Updates We reserve the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time. Any material changes will be notified through our website or by other appropriate means.
If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or the use of Google AdSense on our website, please feel free to contact us via our email address.
